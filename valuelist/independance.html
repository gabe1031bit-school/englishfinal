Independance

Independence, alternatively, is all about standing on one's own feet. People who prioritize this value tend to pursue self-reliance and independence, take personal accountability, and make their own decisions based on their beliefs. This strengthens one's beliefs in oneself, pushes for self-reliance, develops problem-solving skills and boosts confidence. Taking the sole responsibility of what we say, do, think and achieve enhances the spirit of being able to support oneself and lead a better future.
