Frugality

Frugality is about managing one's resources such as money, time, and property intelligently. Those who embrace frugality focus on not wasting things and try to assess properly the need to buy certain items and the extent to which one has to spend on them. Nevertheless, it doesn't necessarily mean not spending on anything, but getting optimum utility out of assets and longevity. It helps maintain a steady finances, look after the environment and make decisions with a future perspective in mind.
