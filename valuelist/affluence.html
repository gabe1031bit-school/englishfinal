Affluence

Affluence is all about having money and the nice things that come with it, like financial security and tons of resources. Folks who value affluence usually put in the work to become successful and create a comfy life for themselves. It lets you buy stuff like good education and healthcare, plus cool experiences you might not get otherwise. Also, feeling wealthy could push you to better your family's and community's lives, or give to charity when you're being responsible with your cash.
