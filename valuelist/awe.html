Awe

Now, awe is very different. It’s that feeling you get when you’re really amazed, like when you look at a breathtaking landscape or hear about some cool new science find. People who love awe are always looking for those special moments that show how vast and wonderful the world is. These stunning experiences can be anything from seeing a comet zoom across the sky to watching someone do an amazing deed for a stranger. Awe not only sparks our curiosity but also keeps us humble. It broadens our view of the world and enriches our lives with new meaning and depth.
