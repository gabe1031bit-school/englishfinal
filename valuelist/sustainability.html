Sustainability

Sustainability entails our efforts to meet our needs while making sure the planet and the prospects of kids are not compromised. People who value sustainability take the long term environmental, societal and economic effects into account. It compels them to adopt green approaches, to conserve and use what they have more responsibly, and also be fairer toward others. Sustainability comes into play in confronting ecological problems, resource management and community development and this value guides them toward keeping equilibrium and taking proper care of the earth, ensuring a better future for everyone.
