Courage

Courage means being willing to face fear, uncertainty, danger, or difficulty even when there are big risks involved. It's not just about being brave in dangerous situations; it also means speaking up for what you believe in, making tough choices, admitting your mistakes, or giving something new a try. Folks who value courage know that growing and moving forward usually mean leaving their comfort zones behind. During hard times, leadership roles, changes, or personal growth spurs, courage is super important 'cause it can boost others' confidence and help people conquer seemingly impossible hurdles.
