Ecstasy

Ecstasy, on the other hand, is about chasing and savoring amazing happiness and excitement. People who rate ecstasy high look for awesome moments that spark joy, inspire them, or make them feel really connected. Whether it's nailing a goal, hanging with folks you love, or getting artistic, it pushes you to enjoy those happy highlights. Not only does it boost up your mood, but it also crafts long-lasting memories, which makes life more fulfilling.
