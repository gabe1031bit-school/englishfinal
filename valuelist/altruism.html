Altruism

Being an altruist is to put the well-being of others first, even at your own expense. It is a matter of acting in someone’s favor out of a genuine desire to see their life improved, driven by love and empathy. Altruism gives us a way to express that concern with kindness and service and not expect anything in return. It is a handy concept to have in mind when you are making choices that will have an impact on people around you, whether that is community work or helping a team membe r succeed. You will find it applies just as well when you are tending to relatives or taking part in events for the best of society.
