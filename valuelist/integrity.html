Integrity

Having integrity is to be true to your principles in an honest and ethical way, no matter what. It means that you will stand by them, even if it is a hard or unpopular thing to do. Someone of integrity does not just put values on paper; that person lives up to them and holds himself to a high moral standard. In the end, that is what gives you credibility; people can count on your actions to be in step with your words. Having integrity is important to be trusted by the community, and with the rise of Artificial Intelligence (AI), it is becoming harder and harder to have that same trust.
