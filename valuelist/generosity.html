Generosity

Generosity is the value that shows the willingness in people to share resources, time, knowledge, attention, or financial, and or other types of support with others. Some types of generosity can involve financial giving, but generosity can extend far beyond money to include mentorship, encouragement, and acts of service. People who have the value of generosity can recognize that helping others can strengthen relationships and improve collective well-being. This value is particularly useful when supporting a team, helping someone overcome challenges, contributing to charitable causes, or creating opportunities for others. Generosity can create positive effects that spread throughout communities and organizations.
